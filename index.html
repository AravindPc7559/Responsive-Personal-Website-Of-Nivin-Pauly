Hi. I'm Nivin Pauly.

Nivin Pauly (born 11 October 1984) is an Indian actor and producer who works in Malayalam films. He is a recipient of several awards including three Filmfare South awards and two Kerala State Film Awards. ... He won the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut for his Tamil debut Neram (2013).
